OpenData and E-Governance

Digital technologies are constantly collecting data about people. This dashboard presents a comparative analysis between the United Nation's Egovernance Index, or the amount of digital services used by governments around the world, and the OpenData Barometer which measures how much of that data is shared open source.

Check out the data and visualizations for each dataset!
